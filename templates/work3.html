003 : Which soccer player is a good deal?

Scenario: You are working with a soccer team. Your manager asked to find out which players are good deal in the market right now. In other words, we want to know which players are getting less paid than their market values.

Data Source: Kaggle FIFA 19 complete player datase

Ste1) Import the Dataset (89 cols & 18207 rows) in Jupyter Notebook

Step2) Clean Data by showing only Columns of Name, Wage, Value, and Difference between the Wage and the Value. Remove '€' and replace 'K', 'M', 'B' with 1000, 1000000, 1000000000 to calculate each Difference.

Step3) Visualize Data with a graph. 'Bokeh' library is used. From the above, We know that Naymar Jr player had the biggest gap between his wage(€29M) and his market value (€118.5M) in 2019.
